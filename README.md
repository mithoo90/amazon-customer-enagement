# amazon-customer-enagement
Cleaned, analyzed, and visualized 42,675 Amazon product listings across 10+ categories using Python and Power BI.
🔧 Tools & Skills
• Python (Pandas, NumPy) — data cleaning & feature engineering
• Power BI — interactive dashboard & business intelligence

📊 Key Highlights
• Processed 42,675 records with 16 features; standardized pricing fields, removed duplicates, and normalized text columns for downstream analysis
• Identified Storage as the highest-discounting category with an average price drop of ~33%, signaling aggressive competitive pricing strategy
• Revealed Laptops & Storage as the top customer engagement categories, collectively driving 20K+ total reviews
• Segmented premium products by category — Printers & Scanners and Networking accounted for ~49% of the top premium product ratio
• Tracked 19M total reviews and an average product rating of 4.47/5 across all categories, indicating strong overall catalog quality
• Calculated average discounted price of ₹127.14, enabling price-sensitivity benchmarking across segments

📈 Business Insights Delivered
• Discount effectiveness analysis to guide promotional strategy
• Customer engagement scoring by category
• Best Seller & Sponsored product distribution mapping
